# **Privacy Policy and Support**

This app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

All data generated by the app (including screen time usage and activity counters) is stored locally on the user's device and is not shared with the developer or any third parties.

The app uses Apple's Screen Time APIs solely to allow users to view and manage their own device usage.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks are used.

For support questions, please open an issue on this page.
